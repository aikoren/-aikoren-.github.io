I have choose some of my current work projects to show you, with a short description below.
If you click on the big images, you will see a new tab with the browser version of my emails, you can check responsiveness with the Google Chrome console.

This is one of my recent projects in ThirdLove, the company I'm working for. I always focus on responsiveness, but for my latests works I'm also focusing on making them bulletproofed for Outlook and Yahoo since sometimes these email clients have rendering issues.

I wanted to show you an email where I use a personalized button. The button over the hero image is going to display the customer's bra size, as "Shop [Bra Size] if we know her size, and it's going to read as "Shop Now" if we don't have her size registered. I use some liquid logic for this function. The email is also responsive and bulletproofed for Outlook and Yahoo.

If you ask me what my favorite emails are, I will say for sure they're the full width ones. We think of this type of emails to be eyecatching when the customers open them in desktop screen size. I think the result is really beautiful. The mobile version doesn't have anything special in these cases, so I'm actually thinking about something to do to make some mobile versions also special as full width desktop versions are.
